child welfare committee

"Every child deserves the chance to thrive. Together, we can ensure their safety, nurture their potential, and create a brighter future through our commitment to child welfare."

Care and Protection: The CWC provides care and protection for children who are neglected, abandoned, or in situations of abuse and exploitation.

Decision Making: The committee has the authority to make decisions regarding the placement of children in need of care and protection in shelters, foster care, or adoption.
